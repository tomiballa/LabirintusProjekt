Labyrinth, the game

Our games main focus is that the player has to discover every room, then find a way out and leave before the time expires.

After you open the game, you can change the language to Hungarian or English, even select a map or save it. Of course, you can start the game too.

Things that our game gives;

  • Changeable difficulty
  • Primely done UI.
  • Entertainment
  • Simple yet great graphics
  • Bug-free gameplay

Movements

  • W - forward
  • A - left
  • S - back
  • D - right
